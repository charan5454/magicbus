About Magic Bus

  • Founded in 1999, Magic Bus empowers underprivileged communities by enabling children to complete their education, gain employment, and lead a life of dignity.
  • We focus on building life skills, ensuring access to education, and creating livelihood opportunities.

Our Vision

  • Create a world where children and young people can achieve their full potential, leading to a life of dignity and self-reliance.

Our Mission

  • Empower children and youth from underserved communities with knowledge, skills, and resources to overcome poverty and secure a better future.

Core Values

  • Empathy: Understanding and addressing the needs of the community.
  • Collaboration: Working with partners and stakeholders to amplify our impact.
  • Integrity: Maintaining transparency and accountability in all our actions.
  • Resilience: Empowering individuals to rise above challenges and achieve success.

Key Programs

  • Childhood to Livelihood Program (C2L): Focuses on education, life skills, and employability.
  • Education Support: Helps children stay in school by addressing barriers like financial constraints and lack of resources.
  • Livelihood Training: Equips young people with vocational skills, job readiness training, and placement support.
  • Community Engagement: Works closely with families, schools, and local governments to create a supportive environment.
  • Sports for Development: Uses sports to teach teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving skills.
